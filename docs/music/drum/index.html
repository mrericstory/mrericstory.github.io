    “Steady, As She Goes” is the debut single by the rock band The Raconteurs from their first album Broken Boy Soldiers. A limited edition, 7-inch, 45 rpm vinyl record, was released in Europe on January 30, 2006, and in North America on March 7, 2006.from wikipedia
